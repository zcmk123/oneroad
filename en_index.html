Old The Belt and Road

   The Silk Road is an ancient land commercial trade route that originated in ancient China, connecting Asia, Africa and Europe. The initial function was to transport ancient Chinese silk, porcelain and other commodities, and later became between the East and the West in the economy, politics , Culture and many other aspects of the main road of communication. Silk Road from the mode of transport, mainly divided into land on the Silk Road and the Silk Road on the sea.Land on Silk Road,refers to the Western Han Dynasty (the previous 202 years - 8 years) Han Emperor sent Zhang Qian to the Western Regions to open up the capital Changan (now Xi'an) as a starting point, the Eastern Han Dynasty to Luoyang City (now Henan Luoyang) as a starting point, by Liangzhou, Jiuquan, Guazhou, Dunhuang, Central Asian countries, Afghanistan, Iran, Iraq, Syria and so on to the Mediterranean, to Rome for the end, a total length of 6440 km.
